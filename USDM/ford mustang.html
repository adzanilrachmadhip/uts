FORD MUSTANG GT

The Ford Mustang GT is a high-performance variant of the legendary Ford Mustang.
This car is powered by a 5.0 liter V8 Crossplane engine that produces strong power,
provides fast acceleration and an iconic exhaust sound. The Mustang GT is known for
its distinctive styling, with a large front grille and impressive design, making it
one of the most recognized muscle cars in the world..
