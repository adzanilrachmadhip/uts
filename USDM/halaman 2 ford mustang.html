ALL FORD MUSTANG GT

Exterior

The exterior of the Ford Mustang GT is characterized by a large, striking
front grille, muscular body design and sharp headlights. Classic details such
as the dividing strip on the hood add an iconic charm to this car, making it
one of the most recognizable muscle cars in the world.

Interior

The interior of the Ford Mustang GT combines classic styling with modern
features, such as comfortable sport seats, an attractive instrument panel
and advanced infotainment technology. The ergonomic and retro-modern design
of the cabin creates an atmosphere that matches the character of a muscle
car, making it a charming car for drivers who value style and performance.

Engine

The Ford Mustang GT has a 5.0-liter V8 Crossplane engine that produces strong
power, often exceeding 400 horsepower, as well as large torque, providing the
high performance typical of muscle cars. The iconic exhaust note from the V8
Crossplane engine adds a distinctive touch to the Mustang GT driving experience.
