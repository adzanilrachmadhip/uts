ALL ABOUT TOYOTA SUPRA MK5

Exterior

The Toyota Supra MK5 has an aerodynamic and sporty exterior design with a
distinctive grille that differentiates it. Its modern and sharp appearance
is emphasized by LED lights and elements such as large alloy wheels and
a rear spoiler..

Interior

The interior of the Toyota Supra MK5 features comfortable sports seats and a
futuristic dashboard design with a large touchscreen, creating a modern feel.
High-quality materials are used in the cabin, providing a premium touch that
suits the character of this sports car.

Engine

The Toyota Supra MK5 is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 ​​engine that
produces impressive power, often exceeding 300 to 380 horsepower, as well as strong
torque, providing high performance that makes it a fun-to-drive sports car. This
engine is paired with a smooth automatic transmission and is available with all-wheel
drive (AWD) technology for better handling in various road conditions.
